Account & Data Deletion

How to delete your BibliaTab Notebook account and data

Last updated: July 17, 2026

You can delete your BibliaTab Notebook account and all associated data at any time, directly inside the app. This page explains exactly how, and what is removed.

01 Delete your account from inside the app

  1. Open BibliaTab Notebook on your device.
  2. Go to Settings (from the Profile tab, or the gear icon).
  3. Under Account, tap Delete Account.
  4. Read the confirmation, type DELETE to confirm, then tap Delete forever.
This is immediate and permanent. Your account and its data are removed right away — there is no recovery. Export your notes first (Settings → Export Notes) if you want to keep a copy.

02 Or request deletion by email

If you can't access the app, email snoubsdevstudio@gmail.com from the address registered to your account, with the subject "Delete my account". We will verify ownership and delete the account within 30 days.

04 Deleting some data without deleting your account

You don't have to delete your whole account to remove data:

  • Delete an individual note, notebook or to-do at any time.
  • Empty Trash (Profile → Trash) to permanently remove deleted notes.
  • Settings → Clear Trash permanently removes all trashed notes.
  • Turn off cloud sync/backup to stop any further data leaving your device.

05 Cancel your subscription first

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel a Google Play subscription. Cancel it in the Google Play Store → Subscriptions before deleting, to avoid further billing.
